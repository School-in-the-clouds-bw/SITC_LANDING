About Us

School in the Cloud is a platform that trains senior volunteers to teach students in a group or individual setting. This helps kids in communities with high student to teacher ratios.

Helping the Community

Our platform connects members of the community togeather, helping community members to give back and share thier knowledge with students in need.


Student Success

Our aim is to help close the achievement gap by connecting students with available, qualified volunteer mentors. Everyone deserves to have acess to all the resources they need to succeed.

It also provides retired volunteers a sense of purpose and meaning in their day to day life when they find themselves with more free time. The platform also connects volunteers with the students.
